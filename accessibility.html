Accessibility

What to know about reaching and moving through the house.

Home · Accessibility

The Way Inn is a 250-year-old stone house in the old city, and we want to be honest about access so you can plan well.

The old city is reached on foot through stone alleys, and there are steps down to the inn and between some of the suites and courtyards. Reception will meet you, guide you from the nearby public parking and help carry luggage.

If mobility is a concern, please call us before booking — we'll talk through which suites are easiest to reach and how to make your stay comfortable.
