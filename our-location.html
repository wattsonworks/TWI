Our Location

In the heart of the old city, two minutes from the centre.

Home · Our Location

The Way Inn is at Simtat Yud-Zayin 23, in the Artists' Quarter of old Safed — two minutes' walk from the centre, reached on foot through the stone alleys.

There are steps down to the inn; reception will guide you to the nearby public parking and help with luggage. From Tel Aviv it is about a 2.5-hour drive; from the Galilee, much less.
