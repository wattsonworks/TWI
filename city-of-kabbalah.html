Safed, where Jewish mysticism came of age.

Home · City of Kabbalah

Safed is the city of Kabbalah — in the 16th century the great mystics, among them the Ari, gathered here and reshaped Jewish thought. The Tree of Life that names our suites was drawn in these alleys.

Staying at The Way Inn places you inside that story — the Flower of Life on our courtyard wall, the Sephirot on our doors, and the same blue light on the windows.
